🌿 Mustard Farming Guide for Farmers

Comprehensive Information for Successful Mustard Cultivation

🌱 Growing Period

Mustard takes 90 to 120 days to mature, depending on the variety and climate.

💰 Current Market Price

The average market price of mustard seeds is around ₹60–₹70 per kg, depending on quality and location.

🌡️ Suitable Temperature

Mustard grows best in temperatures between 10°C to 25°C. It is ideal for cool and dry weather.

📈 Market Demand

Mustard is in high demand for oil extraction, spices, and condiment industries, especially in northern India.

🌾 Crop Type

Mustard is a Rabi crop, sown in October–November and harvested by February–March.

✨ Additional Tips

  • Grows well in loamy or sandy loam soil with good drainage
  • Maintain pH between 6.0 and 7.5
  • Apply proper irrigation during flowering and pod filling
  • Use certified seeds and treat them before sowing
  • Protect from aphids and white rust through timely spraying
