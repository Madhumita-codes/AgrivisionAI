🧅 Onion Farming Guide for Farmers

All essential details to grow and sell onions successfully

🌱 Growing Period

Onions take about 90 to 150 days from sowing to harvesting depending on the variety.

💰 Current Market Price

The market price for onions ranges from ₹15–₹40 per kg based on season and demand.

🌡️ Suitable Temperature

Onions thrive in temperatures between 13°C to 25°C and require cool weather during early growth.

📈 Market Demand

Onions have steady and high demand across India for household and commercial cooking purposes.

🌾 Crop Type

Onion is a Rabi and Kharif crop. Rabi onions are sown in October–November; Kharif onions in June–July.

✨ Additional Tips

  • Requires well-drained loamy soil with pH 6.0–7.0
  • Apply phosphorus and potassium at land preparation stage
  • Irrigate properly at bulb formation stage
  • Use disease-resistant varieties like Bhima Super and NHRDF Red
  • Control pests like thrips and onion fly using safe insecticides
