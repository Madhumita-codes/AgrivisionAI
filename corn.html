🌽 Corn Farming Guide for Farmers

Everything you need to know about growing and selling corn

🌱 Growing Period

Corn takes approximately 90 to 120 days to reach maturity, depending on the variety and climate conditions.

💰 Current Market Price

The current market price of corn is ₹20-₹25 per kg (prices may vary by region and demand).

🌡️ Suitable Temperature

Corn grows best in temperatures between 18°C and 27°C. Avoid planting during frost or extreme heat.

📈 Market Demand

Corn has a high demand in industries like food processing, cattle feed, ethanol production, and popcorn manufacturing.

🌾 Crop Type

Corn is a Kharif crop in India, generally sown during the monsoon season (June–July) and harvested in October–November.

✨ Additional Tips

  • Use well-drained loamy soil with pH 5.5-7.0
  • Apply balanced fertilizers (NPK ratio 2:1:1)
  • Practice crop rotation to reduce pests
  • Ensure proper irrigation during tasseling and silking stages
